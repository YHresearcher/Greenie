About Greenie

Born from a refusal to accept vague food promises.

Greenie began after real factory visits showed a difficult truth: too many food brands used beautiful words while hiding unclear processes behind the label.

At the same time, Vietnamese agricultural products were still being sold as low-value raw materials, while other markets processed them deeper and captured the value. Greenie was created to change that path with transparency, documentation, and disciplined selection.

We do not simply buy products. We select integrity.
