Origin: Quang Ngai, Vietnam

Duong Phoi:
A 400-Year Artisan Legacy

From the vast sugarcane fields of Quang Ngai, we bring you "Duong Phoi"—a traditional Vietnamese cane sugar crafted through four centuries of generational wisdom. Pure, preservative-free, and unrefined.

Alluvial Soil & Central Coast Sun

Quang Ngai, where sugarcane fields stretch along the Tra Khuc River, enjoys a distinctive tropical monsoon climate—year-round warmth and abundant rainfall create ideal conditions for sugarcane cultivation.

The nutrient-rich alluvial soil, deposited over millennia by river systems, provides exceptional mineral content that gives Quang Ngai sugarcane its unmistakable deep sweetness.

Our farmers maintain traditional cultivation methods without chemical inputs, ensuring every stalk reaches its peak natural sweetness.

Technical Note

Natural sucrose content is 18% higher than standard sugarcane varieties due to unique soil and climate conditions.

The 400-Year Artisan Process

From traditional stone-press milling to slow-fire copper kettle reduction, every stage is an art form passed from generation to generation.

I. Fresh Pressing

Sugarcane is harvested at peak ripeness and pressed within hours using traditional granite rollers to preserve natural flavor and nutrients.

II. Slow-Fire Reduction

The juice is reduced in traditional copper kettles over a slow wood fire for 4-6 hours, stirred continuously until reaching perfect consistency.

III. Natural Crystallization

The sugar is poured into wooden molds, cooled naturally, and sun-dried. No chemicals, no preservatives—only pure, unrefined nature.

Flavor Profile

Duong Phoi carries the distinctive aroma of fresh sugarcane with a gentle, clean sweetness that never cloys. When dissolved, it creates a natural amber-gold hue—the signature of unbleached, unrefined purity.

Culinary Versatility

Trusted by Asian and European chefs, Duong Phoi is the perfect choice for premium mixology, artisan baking, and health-conscious cuisine. Its complex molasses notes elevate any application.
